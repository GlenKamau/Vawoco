From Rags to Riches

Textile waste is choking our planet, but in the hands of our upcycling team, old clothes become beautiful, useful, and sellable products.

The Power of Upcycling Fashion.

At VAWOCO, we collect worn-out garments, redesign and stitch them into jeans bags, children’s wear, aprons, and home accessories. These creations aren’t just eco-friendly—they’re affordable alternatives for families who can’t access fast fashion

Even better, we train and certify youth in phone repair, equipping them with a practical skill for life. A repaired phone is more than just a gadget—it’s opportunity, income, and connection.

We’ve partnered with tailors, youth groups, and women’s collectives to make upcycling a source of both income and dignity.

Every piece sold empties a landfill and fills a household with pride. Shop consciously—shop circular.
